clic-o-matic

silently observe click behaviour of your visitors with heatmap and click-o-graph
H

Heatmap

Using clic-o-matic, you can view the heatmap of clicks by the user on various webpages of your website. Heatmaps let you identify the hot-spots of attraction from users viewpoint.
C

Click-o-Graph

There is also an option to view the a graph with exact positions marked where the users visiting your website clicked.
